Computers, the cornerstone of modern technology, have evolved dramatically from their initial invention.
Initially designed for complex calculations, they now power the digital world, influencing every aspect of our lives.
Their capabilities span from basic data processing to enabling advanced artificial intelligence. The heart of a computer lies in its central processing unit (CPU),
which executes instructions from software applications. Memory components, like RAM and storage drives, store and retrieve data.
Connectivity through the internet has transformed computers into global communication hubs, facilitating information exchange and digital collaboration.
